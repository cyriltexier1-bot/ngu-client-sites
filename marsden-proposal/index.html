Marketing Strategy

The NGU Three-Phase Campaign

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–2

Off-Market: Build Demand Before the World Knows

  • Target: NGU's internal database of 300+ pre-qualified 3-bedroom buyers across South East QLD
  • VIP email broadcast + personal calls to active investors and downsizers
  • No public exposure — preserves price integrity and creates artificial scarcity
  • Goal: 3–4 serious written enquiries within 14 days

Phase 2 · Week 3

Soft Launch: Pre-Qualified Buyers Only

  • Twilight VIP inspection for top 10 interested parties (pre-qualified only)
  • Controlled private showings — no tyre-kickers, no time wasted
  • If premium offer secured: accept and settle in 30 days
  • Buyer competition begins here — before you spend a dollar on portal advertising

Phase 3 · Week 4+

Full Market: Maximum Competition via Auction

  • Live on realestate.com.au Premiere+, Domain.com Premium, and all national portals
  • Professional photography, video walkthrough, and digital floor plans
  • Social media campaign targeting investors, families, and DINK downsizers
  • Saturday open homes 9–10am for 2–3 consecutive weekends
  • Result: Competitive pressure typically adds 12–18% above reserve at auction
