Marketing Strategy

The NGU Approach: Three-Phase Campaign

Phase 1: Off-Market (Weeks 1-2)

Goal: Create early competition, preserve price integrity

  • Target: Internal database of 300+ qualified 3-bedroom buyers
  • Strategy: VIP email broadcast + one-on-one calls to investors & downsizers
  • Expected outcome: 3-4 serious inquiries by week 2
  • Benefit: Identify serious buyers willing to act fast without public exposure

Phase 2: Soft Launch (Week 3)

Goal: Create exclusivity and urgency with pre-qualified buyers

  • VIP twilight inspection for top 10 interested parties
  • Private inspections with pre-qualified buyers only
  • Objective: Negotiate strong off-market offer
  • Timeline: If premium offer secured → settle within 30 days

Phase 3: Full Market (Week 4+)

Goal: If Phase 2 doesn't yield, go to auction for maximum competition

  • Launch on realestate.com.au, Domain, and national portals
  • Social media campaign targeting investor + downsizer networks
  • Professional photography, video, and floor plans
  • Open homes: Saturdays 9-10am (2-3 consecutive weekends)
  • Result: Competitive pressure typically yields 12-15% premium in this market
