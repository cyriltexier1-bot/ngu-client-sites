## OBJECTION HANDLING SCRIPTS

### "I need time to think about it"
Get to the core issue. Write down and tick off in front of them:
> Process → Off Market option → Launch Online → Plan C → Energy → Enthusiasm → Local Agent → Reviews → Fee

### "I'll let you know"
> "What's holding you back from moving forward? You've said you want to sell — I want to sell it. What do I have to do to get your business?"

### "I have other agents coming out this week"
> "Fantastic — which agents? I'd like to compare apples with apples." Pull up their REA profile in front of them and compare stats side by side.

### "I don't need to sell"
> "But if the price is right, as we discussed, you would sell? Let me show you current market buyers first."

### "I can wait / I'm not in a rush"
> Go back to the calendar close. Ask them for a timeline. Offer settlement flexibility or rent-back options. Circle back: "We can test the water first — no cost involved, market it with me."

### "Your fees are too high"
> "My fees are $0 and free if I don't sell it. Let me ask — what do you feel is a fair fee?"
> Calculate in front of them: e.g. $1M × 2% = $20,000 vs $1M × 2.5% = $25,000.
> "All I need to do is negotiate an extra $5,000 from the buyer and my fee is covered — and anything above that means you're better off. Cheap fees don't mean more money in your pocket. A higher sale price does."

### "I've heard negative things about you"
> Never bag other agents. Pull up RateMyAgent and REA pages and read reviews aloud. Play video testimonials. Offer to prepare a review booklet. Then: "Now that this is addressed, shall we move forward?"

### "I'm not signing today"
> Circle back to uncovering the real objection. Work through the checklist above with them.

## SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY

### Bio Guidelines (All Platforms)
- Consistent professional headshot across all platforms
- Same colour scheme as REA profile
- Use an emoji before each bio line
- Include: Job title + agency (tag company page), suburbs you service, contact number, awards/credentials, short tagline, one link (website/listings)

### 12 Content Ideas (Post 1–2x Daily)
1. Property listing (internal photos)
2. Property listing video (you presenting)
3. Sold-by photos (buyer & seller)
4. Sold-by video testimonials
5. Live or recorded auction videos
6. Reviews (RateMyAgent / realestate.com.au)
7. Weekly market wrap-up video
8. Sponsorship photos (teams, schools)
9. Community initiatives
10. Charity events & donations
11. Spotlight local businesses (café, pizza shop)
12. Behind-the-scenes photos of listings

## WEEKLY WRAP-UP VIDEO SCRIPT OUTLINE

**Format:** 2–3 minutes, pre-recorded, uploaded weekly

1. Introduce yourself, your company and your core suburb/surroundings
2. Open home numbers this week
3. What's currently on the market for sale
4. What's coming soon (listed but not yet online)
5. Recent sales with stories
6. Office sales this month
7. Upcoming auctions (addresses + dates)
8. Market trends in your area
9. Interest rates or economic stats update
10. "I currently have a buyer looking for..." (criteria)
