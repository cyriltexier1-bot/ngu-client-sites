Brisbane —
The Investment Case

Brisbane is not speculative — it is a market with structural tailwinds that make it one of the most compelling investment destinations in the country. Here's why now.

01

8–10% Growth Forecast 2026

SQM Research and Propertyology both project strong Brisbane apartment growth for 2026 — driven by chronic supply shortages and accelerating population migration.

02

Sub-1% Vacancy Rates

Inner-city precincts including Spring Hill, Kangaroo Point, and South Brisbane are recording vacancy rates below 1%. Investors rarely wait between tenants.

03

$6B+ Olympic Infrastructure

The 2032 Brisbane Olympics is catalysing a $6 billion+ infrastructure spend — transport, amenity, and urban renewal that directly benefits inner-city apartment values.

04

1,700+ New Residents Per Week

South-East Queensland continues to attract 1,700+ new residents weekly — a structural demand driver that is not slowing. Supply cannot keep pace.
