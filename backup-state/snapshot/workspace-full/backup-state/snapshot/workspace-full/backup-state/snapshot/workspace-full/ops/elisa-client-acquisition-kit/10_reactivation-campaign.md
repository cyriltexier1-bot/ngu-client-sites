# Reactivation Campaign (Old Leads)

## Segment
- Leads with no activity in last 60+ days
- Separate by goal: buyer/seller/investor/renter

## 3-touch sequence

### Touch 1 (Day 0)
Subject: Still exploring {{goal}}?

Hi {{first_name}}, quick check-in — are you still looking to {{goal}} this quarter?
If yes, I can send an updated plan for your situation.

### Touch 2 (Day 4)
Hi {{first_name}}, sharing a quick update in your area: {{market_note}}.
Want a tailored shortlist/strategy?

### Touch 3 (Day 10)
Hi {{first_name}}, I’ll pause this for now.
Reply “yes” anytime and I’ll restart with priority.

## KPI
- Reactivated leads / contacted leads
- Target: 8-12% baseline
